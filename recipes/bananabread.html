Banana Bread Recipe

This is my own recipe that I learned from my mom!

It's fluffy, moist, and full of flavor.

It's open to any modifications such as nuts and chocolate chips which can just me mixed into the batter! (Once again, this isn't my picture)

Ingredients

  • 3 Bananas (Better if Ripe)
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • Stick of Butter
  • 1 teaspoon of salt (Not needed if salted butter is used)

Steps

  1. On the stove on medium heat, melt the butter until it is brown and let it cool for a few minutes.
  2. Mash the bananas into a bowl until there aren't any chunks.
  3. Add the butter into the banana graveyard.
  4. Add the eggs, vanilla extract, and brown sugar and mix until combined.
  5. After combined, add the flour, cinnamon, and baking soda and mix until combined.
  6. If the butter wasn't salted, add the salt.
  7. Spray a 9x5 pan with oil and put the mixture into the pan until it is halfway full.
  8. Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.
    • To check if its ready, stick a knife through parts of the bread and if it comes out clean, it's done.
    • Check the center and sides for readiness throughout all of the bread.
  9. Let it cool for 5-10 minutes and enjoy!
