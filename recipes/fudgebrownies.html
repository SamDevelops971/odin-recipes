Fudge Brownie Recipe

It's quick and you can even add your own spin to it such as nuts or chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • Cocoa Powder
  • Flour
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Soda
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Melt 1 and 1/4 stick butter until browned. Set it so the side to cool
  2. In a bowl, mix about a cup of flour, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/2 tsp baking soda. If salted butter was used, salt is not needed.
  3. In a separate bowl, microwave yout chocolate chips for 15-20 seconds and then mix that with the browned butter.
  4. Mix the butter and chocolate mixture with 1/4 cup cocoa powder.
  5. Mix in 2 eggs, vanilla extract, and 1 1/4 cup brown sugar.
  6. Add the flour into the chocolate mixture and fold it in with a spatula
  7. Spray an 8x8 pan with oil and bake for 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
