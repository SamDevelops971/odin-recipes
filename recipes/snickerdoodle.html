Snickerdoodle Recipe

This recipe I have been trying to perfect and even now it is nowhere near where I want them to be.

I started off with an online recipe then slowly began tweaking it to my liking.

However, they are fluffy, delicious, and a great sweet treat to bring to any event!

P.S This is not my picture, but this recipe will look 10x better than this one.

Ingredients for Cookie Dough (8-12 Cookies)

  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Cream of Tartar
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt

Ingredients for Cinnamon Sugar

  • Cinnamon
  • Granulated Sugar

Preparation Time: 30 Minutes

Bake Time: 11-13 Minutes

Steps

    Cookie Dough

    I recommend preheating oven to 350 before starting!

  1. Melt a Stick, or half a cup, of butter.

    • I recommened browning butter. (Cook on stove for a few minutes until butter browns. Let it cool for a few minutes as well.)

  2. Mix melted butter w/ half a cup of brown sugar and 1/2 cup granulated sugar.
  3. Add however much vanilla extract you want. No more than 3 caps.
  4. Mix in one egg.
  5. Mix in 3 cups of flour. Mix one cup before moving onto the next and stop if the texture is smooth, but not too doughey.
  6. Mix in a pinch of salt. (I do not recommend if salted butter is used)
  7. Mix in 2 tablespoons of cream of tartar
  8. Mix in 3 teaspoons of baking soda.

    9. Cinnamon sugar

    • In a separate bowl, mix a quarter cup of granulated sugar w/ an eigth cup cinnamon.

  9. Once cinnamon sugar is made, form the dough into balls and roll them in the cinnamon sugar
  10. If not done already, set the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  11. Lastly, place on a baking pan and bake for 11-13 minutes until slightly brown and expanded.

    • I recommend either buttering or lightly spraying the pan w/ oil.

  12. Let them cool after removing them from the oven, and enjoy!
